Our Process

Our products are constantly evolving. Boundaries are pushed and failures are celebrated. The drive to experiment is what fuels our passion and transforms our work into a labor of love.

Crafting eyewear at Shwood is a meticulous process that blends traditional techniques with innovative craftsmanship. Our journey begins with sourcing the finest sustainable materials, ensuring that each piece tells a story of quality and sustainability.

Once the materials are curated, our skilled artisans meticulously shape and mold each component by hand, ensuring precision and attention to detail at every step. From carving the frames to polishing the lenses, every stage of production is imbued with our commitment to excellence.

But our process doesn't stop there. We believe in pushing boundaries and exploring new possibilities. Our design team is constantly experimenting with new techniques and materials, pushing the limits of what's possible in eyewear design.

Finally, each pair of Shwood glasses undergoes rigorous quality control checks to ensure that they meet our exacting standards. It's this dedication to craftsmanship and innovation that sets Shwood apart, creating eyewear that's not just stylish, but truly exceptional.
